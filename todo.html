EVENTS

The Castle Festival is an event held continuously in autumn and spring at the Kumamoto Castle Ruins, a special historical site. This is an opportunity for many people from Japan and abroad to come into contact with Japanese culture at Kumamoto Castle, which has been cultivated in history and tradition.

Started about 40 years ago to create an event in which all citizens can enjoy participating and is now the largest festival in Kumamoto. The festival’s highlight is the So Odori, during which more than 5,000 people dance together around the city.
