FOOD

Karashi Renkon

Made from lotus root stuffed with Japanese mustard and miso paste, the covered batter made from egg and flour and fried.

Aka Beef Bowl

Slices of medium-rare beef steak are luxuriously served on rice, topped with a half-boiled egg. A wagyu beef is raised in the lush nature of Aso, Kumamoto.

Ikinari Dango

Steamed flattened kneaded flour filled with mashed sweet potato and Azuki red bean paste. Known as a traditional local dessert that has been prepared by ordinary families for a long time.
